◆ How it's made

From hide to heirloom, four hands.

Every piece on this page passes through four pairs of hands before it reaches you. Empat pasang tangan, satu barang.

I

Select the hide

Eca hand-picks each full-grain hide at our tannery in Garut. One hide makes two bags.

II

Cut & skive

Pattern is cut by hand, edges skived to 1.5mm for clean saddle-stitched seams.

III

Stitch by hand

Two needles, waxed thread. A medium tote takes eight hours of stitching.

IV

Burnish & finish

Edges are slicked with gum tragacanth until they shine. Hardware set by hand.
