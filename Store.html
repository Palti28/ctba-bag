◆ Materials & Care · Bahan & Perawatan

Leather that ages well. So should the knowledge.

01 / FULL-GRAIN
I.

The Hide Kulit

Vegetable-tanned full-grain from Garut. We use only the top 1.8mm layer — the one that carries the grain, scars, and character that synthetic leather will never have.

02 / THREAD
II.

The Stitch Jahitan

Waxed linen thread, hand-pulled through every hole with two needles — the saddle stitch. It takes 4× longer than a machine seam and lasts a generation.

03 / RITUAL
III.

The Care Perawatan

Wipe with a dry cloth weekly. Condition every 3–4 months with the balm in your care kit. Avoid direct sun. Scratches are memory — let them be.
